Welcome to Heritage Explorer

Sri Lanka, the Pearl of the Indian Ocean, is home to eight magnificent UNESCO World Heritage Sites that showcase thousands of years of rich cultural and natural history.

Our platform helps you discover and learn about the most iconic heritage sites including ancient cities, colonial fortresses, sacred temples, and breathtaking natural landscapes. Embark on a journey through time and explore the wonders that make Sri Lanka truly remarkable.
